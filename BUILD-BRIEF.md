## Technical Requirements for Shopify

### Theme
- Custom Dawn 2.0-based theme, heavily modified
- Single product focus, homepage IS the product page
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Interactive, animated experience throughout (see CLAUDE-CODE-PROMPT.md for full specs)

### Design Direction
- Premium DTC brand experience, not a template
- 3D product viewer (Three.js) in hero section
- GSAP + ScrollTrigger for scroll-driven animations
- Glass-morphism cards, purple glow effects, custom cursor
- Parallax backgrounds, hover micro-interactions on all elements
- Pinned/scroll-locked education section
- Auto-scrolling review carousel
- Count-up stat animations, staggered reveals, SVG line-draw animations
- Every section has at least one interactive or animated element
- Reduced motion media query fallback for accessibility

### Tech Stack
- Three.js (CDN) for 3D product rendering
- GSAP + ScrollTrigger (CDN) for animations
- Vanilla JS only (no jQuery, no Bootstrap)
- CSS custom properties for theming
- Intersection Observer for scroll triggers

### Apps to Consider
- Reviews: Judge.me or Loox
- Email capture: Klaviyo or Mailchimp
- SEO: SEO Manager or Smart SEO
- Currency: auto-detect for UK (GBP default)

### Shipping
- UK-based fulfilment
- Free delivery messaging prominent

### Payment
- Shopify Payments (Stripe)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay / Google Pay
